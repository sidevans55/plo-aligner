Canadian Program Framework

The CPF provides a comprehensive framework for biological sciences education, organized around three core themes:

Knowledge

Core biological concepts, interdisciplinary knowledge, and understanding of scientific methodology.

Skills

Experimental design, data analysis, communication, collaboration, and technical competencies.

Values

Ethical responsibility, societal impact awareness, and commitment to lifelong learning.
